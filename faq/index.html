Frequently Asked Questions

What is E-Visa Process?

An e-Visa is an official authorization document in soft PDF format permitting entry into and travel within foreign land. Applicants obtain their visas electronically after entering required information online and making payments by a credit or debit card.

Applicant can fill an application form, re-correct or modify application details, track visa status, 24*7 online support are few advantages of the e-Visa process.

How to obtain a Visa from e-Visa Process?
  • Visit www.ievisa.com
  • Select nationality and currently living in.
  • Select visa types.
  • Fill an Application Form
  • Complete your transaction by making payment.
  • Receive an email confirmation.
What are the documents required to apply for an e-Visa?

Documents required may change basis your nationality.

Basic Required Documents:
  • Colored scanned copy of the passport and it should be valid for 6 months from the arrival date.
  • International travelers should have a return ticket.
  • Colored Scanned copy of your front facing photograph with white background.
Can ieVisa get the visa approved, if I am a Diplomatic/Official passport holder?
  • No, you can only apply with normal passport.
Can ieVisa get the visa approved, if I have my name on Parent’s passport or if I am a travel document holder?
  • No, we can’t get the visa approved.
How long should a passport be valid for travel?

It should be 6 months from the date of arrival.

How to Make Payment at ieVisa?

ieVisa currently has two payment gateways to offer, PayPal and PayTabs. Select one – Fill your card details and complete the transaction.

Are Payments Secure at ieVisa?

Yes, it’s safe to transact!

PayPal and PayTabs are globally acclaimed as secure payment gateway, worldwide people use it for the transactions online. ieVisa.com is SSL secured and integrated both the gateways for the applicant’s peace of mind.

How long does it take for a visa to be approved?

To organize your visa, we'll simply need to confirm - which option (Regular or Express) you would prefer.

  • Regular service takes 48-72 hours
  • Express service takes 24 working hours.
